ACHILLES

Privacy Policy — Effective April 3, 2026

Overview

Achilles ("the App") is a fitness, nutrition, and macro tracking application for iOS. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App handles and how.

Data Collection

Achilles does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information you enter — including your profile, meals, workouts, and weight entries — is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.

No Network Activity

The App makes no network requests. It does not connect to any server, API, cloud database, or third-party service. Your data never leaves your device.

No Tracking

Achilles does not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking frameworks. We do not collect device identifiers, usage data, or behavioral metrics.

No Account Required

The App does not require account creation, sign-in, or any form of authentication. All data is local to your device.

Data Storage and Deletion

  • All data is stored locally on your device.
  • Deleting the App removes all associated data permanently.
  • No backups are made to external servers.

Third-Party Services

Achilles does not integrate with any third-party services for data collection or processing. Crash reporting data may be collected by Apple if you have opted in to share diagnostics with app developers through your device settings.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Since no data is collected from any user, no special provisions are necessary.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a revised effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at: support@achilles.app
